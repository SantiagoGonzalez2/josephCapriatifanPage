Biography

Joseph Capriati was born in 1987 and is another of those new figures emerging from the southern Italian scene (specifically, Caserta). Discover techno back in 2003, after attending an event with Dave Clarke and Billy Nasty. He begins to make his first steps with a computer until his first publications in 2007, "C'est La Vie" and "Microbiotik". From there, Capriati's projection increases throughout the world. Joseph's passion and conviction are on full display in the way he has made and played music. Whether it's having the confidence to release a debut album of ambient and drum & bass songs, or the discipline and creative freedom that convinced Adam Beyer to release two massive EPs on his Drumcode label, or the versatility and insight that characterizes Josephs in your DJ sets and allows you to connect with crowds from Europe, South America, Asia, North America and South Africa.
